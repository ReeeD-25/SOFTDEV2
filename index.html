About OptiCore

"Where Intelligence Meets Academic Efficiency"

OptiCore is a smart study load optimization platform that applies algorithmic scheduling techniques to generate efficient and balanced academic timetables. The system evaluates user-defined constraints such as subject weight, task urgency, and available study hours to compute an optimized distribution of workload.

Designed with a system-oriented architecture, OptiCore aims to reduce cognitive overload, improve time management, and enhance academic productivity through intelligent automation.
